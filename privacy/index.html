Privacy Policy

FateAtlas is designed to provide BaZi, Western astrology, zodiac fortune, and AI interpretation features while keeping personal context limited to what is needed for the reading experience.

Information You Provide

The app may ask for birth date, birth time, birth location, gender or identity context, and questions you choose to ask. This information is used as context for generating interpretations and chart-related insights.

How Birth Information Is Used

Birth information is used only as interpretive context for BaZi, Western astrology, zodiac fortune, and AI-generated readings. FateAtlas and its developer do not save birth information for profiling, do not sell it, and do not use it to interfere with your personal decisions.

AI Interpretation

When you request an AI interpretation, relevant context may be processed to create the response. AI output is reflective and informational. It should not be treated as professional advice or as a substitute for your own judgment.

Data Sale and Advertising

FateAtlas does not sell personal information. The app is not designed to build advertising profiles from your birth information or reading history.

Decision-Making

FateAtlas does not make decisions for you and does not intervene in your choices. You are responsible for how you interpret and act on any reading.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact support through the Support page.
