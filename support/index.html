Support / Contact

Need help with FateAtlas?

For product questions, account questions, privacy requests, or issue reports, contact the FateAtlas team by email.

support@fateatlas.aben.io

What should I include?

Describe the issue, the app version if available, your device model, and the steps that led to the problem. Do not send passwords or sensitive account credentials.

Can readings decide for me?

No. FateAtlas provides interpretive context and reflective prompts. It does not make decisions, guarantee outcomes, or replace professional advice.

Privacy questions

Use the same support address for privacy questions or deletion requests. See the Privacy Policy for how birth information is handled.
